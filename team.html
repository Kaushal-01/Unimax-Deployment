Choosing the Right Investigative Provider


When selecting an investigative provider, it's crucial to ask: Who will conduct my investigation? At UNIMAX, we prioritize transparency by providing detailed educational and background information on all our field staff upfront.

We exclusively employ full-time professionals who are committed to exceeding client expectations. Unlike many competitors, we do not engage part-time or contract staff. Our investigative team boasts diverse expertise in investigation, claims handling, and other pertinent fields.

Strategically located across the country, our investigators live in the areas they serve, giving them an invaluable understanding of local laws, customs, contacts, resources, and geography. In an industry often characterized by high turnover, our ability to cultivate a team of dedicated career professionals is evident in the profiles of our investigators.
