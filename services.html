Health Insurance Claims Verification


UNIMAX partners with leading players in the insurance industry to foster a fact-based claims investigation environment. With expert personnel and advanced technology, we assist clients in achieving quicker and more efficient claim reimbursements. Our outsourcing solutions encompass three primary types of insurance: Health, Life, and General.

Our dedicated team for health insurance claims investigation is well-versed in various claims scenarios, including health issues and disease management. This expertise ensures precise evaluations of each claim, preventing losses from fraudulent or miscalculated claims.

Recognizing that claims processing heavily relies on the information provided by claimants, UNIMAX emphasizes the importance of complete and accurate data submissions.

Life Insurance


UNIMAX delivers comprehensive verification and processing solutions for the life insurance sector, enabling companies to determine the correct claim amounts and expedite processes through our extensive network and expertise. Our team collaborates with local entities, including hospitals and law enforcement, to ensure thorough analysis of legal and medical documents to prevent fraud.

The threat of false claims is significant, especially in life insurance, where the amounts involved can be substantial. UNIMAX safeguards insurers' interests by thoroughly verifying each claim, utilizing professional expertise, technology, and local networks to provide reliable claims verification services.

Our verification process begins with collecting critical information from the deceased's immediate family, including:
  • Proposal Form
  • Age Verification
  • Death Certificate
  • Claim Forms
  • Case Sheets
    •

Key focus areas include establishing the cause of death or accident, verifying the legitimacy of claimants, and ensuring all necessary legal and medical documentation is authentic.

General Insurance


UNIMAX offers verification and processing solutions tailored for the general insurance sector. Our comprehensive services aid companies in accurately determining claim amounts, fostering operational efficiency in both cost and time. As insurance requirements expand to cover more assets—such as vehicles, travel, and home loans—accurate claims verification becomes increasingly crucial.

Our investigation process involves collecting the following information from claimants:
  • Proposal Form
  • Proof of Asset Ownership
  • First Information Report (FIR)
  • Claim Forms
  • Case Sheets
Key assurance areas in our general insurance services include:
  • Asset and Site Verification: We provide prompt inspections and verifications across our extensive nationwide network.
  • Documentation Review: Our team consists of experts from investigative, forensic, and legal backgrounds who meticulously evaluate all relevant documents to ensure accurate asset valuations.
  • Intent and Ownership Verification: We establish the genuine intent behind claims and verify asset ownership, mitigating the risk of inflated claims.

UNIMAX's robust understanding of documentation norms and regulations across various Indian states, combined with our own nationwide network, positions us as a leader in expediting claims processing through our expertise in IT and analytics.
